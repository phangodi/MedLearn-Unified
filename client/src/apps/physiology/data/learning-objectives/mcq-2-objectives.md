## 28. Oxygen transport in blood

- **Describe how the oxyhemoglobin dissociation curve is affected by changes in pCO2 (Bohr-effect), plasma pH and red blood cell 2,3-DPG concentration. Explain the functional significance of these changes**
- Give 5 causes of hypoxia. **Reference values:** HbA P50: 26 mmHg; arterial/mixed venous blood oxygen saturation: 97-98/75%; Hufner-number: 1.34 ml/g; oxygen concentration in arterial/venous blood: 200/150 ml/l; arteriovenous oxygen difference (AVDO2): 50 ml/l; resting oxygen uptake/metabolic rate: 250-280 ml/min
